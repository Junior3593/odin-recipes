How to Prepare Ramen

  1. Step 1: Make the Broth
    In a large stockpot, simmer pork bones (for tonkotsu), or chicken carcasses, aromatics (garlic, ginger, onions), and kombu (seaweed) for depth.
    Skim impurities regularly.
    Strain and reserve the clear, flavorful broth.
  2. Step 2: Prepare the Tare (Flavor Base)
    Combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, dried fish (like bonito flakes), and kombu, simmer gently, then strain.
